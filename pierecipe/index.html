What is a pie?

A pie is a baked dish which is usually made of a pastry dough casing that contains a filling of various sweet or savoury ingredients.

3 Pie types

  • Pecan Pie
  • Apple Pie
  • Pumpkin Pie

Random pie facts

  • There's a town called Pie Town in New Mexico, USA
  • The largest pie made weighed in at 10,540kg
  • The first pie recipe was published by the Romans
